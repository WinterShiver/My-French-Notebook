# A-02-提问方法


## 特殊疑问句

**comment (what, how)**
English | French
---- | ----
Comment tu manges? | What do you eat? / What would you want to eat?
Comment Marie parle? | What did Marie say?


## 疑问词

**où | where**
French | English
---- | ----
Où est le restaurant? | Where is the restaurant?
Ici. | Here.
